Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2025

TruWord Music ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. No Personal Data Collected

TruWord Music does not collect, store, or share any personal data. You do not need to create an account or sign in to use the app.

2. Apple Music API Usage

We use Apple’s MusicKit API to display public music data, such as top Christian albums and tracks. All data is fetched from Apple Music and is not personalized or tied to any specific user.

3. No Ads or Analytics

We do not show third-party advertisements, use cookies, or track any usage through analytics tools.
